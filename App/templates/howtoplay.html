How To Play

| 1 |

Welcome to ShadowCipher!

You goal is to decode the a secret number chosen by your opponent within a limited number of guesses.

Can you crack the code?

| 2 |

To make a guess, enter a digit from 0-9 into each available slot, then press 'SUMBIT'.

Repeat digits (i.e., multiple instances of the same digit) are not allowed, and the first digit cannot be zero (0).

| 3 |

After each guess, clues are given in the form of three (3) distinct values:

Shuriken: The number of correct digits that are in the correct position.

Kunai: The number of correct digits that belong in another position.

Smoke: The number of wholly incorrect digits.

| 4 |

Additional information is given by the colour of of the box surrounding each digit of your previous guess.

Green: The digit has a 'Shuriken' value.

Yellow: The digit has a 'Kunai' value.

Brown: The digit has a 'Smoke' value.

| 5 |

With each guess, use the feedback to refine your strategy and get closer to victory.

| 6 |

Keep guessing and analyzing until you've cracked the code!
